About Us

With the motto that the “World is one Family” the GGKF is dedicated to nurture peace, harmony and happiness by empowering the people with knowledge, skills and human values akin to the needs of the new knowledge society of the age of innovation that has a promise to nurture goodness in plenty and inspire the youth to create a world of bliss and fulfillment. Established by a philanthropic group led by India’s leading lights in academics and research, the GGKF is committed to serve the Humanity and Mother Nature with its focus on quality, human excellence and sustainability.

Aims and Objectives

  • To conduct skill development programs in areas of high employability.
  • Undertake research projects in subjects related to the foundation.
  • Organize and attend national and international conferences concerning its declared objectives.
  • Institute fellowships and awards to recognize academic and research excellence and service to society and humanity.
  • Provide scholarships, fellowships, and financial grants to deserving students and scholars for research projects and higher studies.
  • Carry out any other object of general public utility.
  • Receive financial and non-financial assistance from government, NGOs, national/international agencies, banks, and other legal entities and individuals.
  • Accept donations, grants, presents, gifts, and other offerings in the shape of movable and immovable properties for the attainment of the foundation’s aims and objects.
  • Edit, print, publish, and exhibit books, papers, periodicals, souvenirs, newsletters, and other publications, and undertake various activities for the general public welfare.
  • Cooperate with all such bodies, organizations, and agencies, national/international, pursuing similar goals or open/establish branches at national/international levels to support the cause of world peace and brotherhood.
  • Award deserving persons for their contribution to quality education, environmental sustainability, peace, innovation, and inventions related to the welfare of humanity.
  • Foster innovation, quality, and relevance in education and research to save Mother Earth’s environment for present and future generations.
